Hypothesis High internal temperature makes the patient be transfered Intensive Care Unit
Conclusion

As visiulized on the graphicons above, the correspondance between internal temperature and being sent to the ICU is very low.

Actually, our testing group did not have any recorded cases on patients being sent to the ICU with High internal temperature. Most cases are actually beaing treated in the General hospital floor rather than being ready to lable to go home.

On a more general notice, we can conclude that there is no crucial corespondence between the internal body temperature and being sent to the ICU.
